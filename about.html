The most memorable moments during Daniek Godschalk’s childhood were at home, experimenting with different ingredients on the island countertop, no older than the age of two.

Many years pass and Daniek’s passion for baking grew. The art of harmonising flavours became an undeniable skill.

The recipes we use today are from the family archives. Our ingredients may be published but our baking methods remain a ancestry secret.

With every box we bake being homemade; we guarantee pure delight with every bite.
